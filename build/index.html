FAQ

You may still have questions. We answer them

When can I use the finished product?
We provide a full cycle of software development, deployment and post-release support. You'll get a functional, scalable and secure product, perfect for business. MVP in 90 days.
I have an unfinished application. Can you refine it?
Absolutely! We understand that sometimes projects may get stuck or need further refinement. Our team of experienced developers and consultants can assist you in refining and completing your application. Whether you need help with design improvements, feature enhancements, or bug fixing, we can work closely with you to bring your application to completion.
How much does it cost to develop a website?
The cost of developing a website can vary depending on several factors, including the complexity of the project, the desired features and functionality, the design requirements, and the expected timeline. To provide you with an accurate cost estimate, we would need to gather more details about your specific website project. We offer customized solutions tailored to your needs and budget, so feel free to reach out to us with your project requirements, and we'll be happy to provide you with a detailed cost estimate.
Do you give a warranty on the mobile app?
Yes, we provide a warranty on the mobile apps we develop. Our warranty ensures that the app will be free from defects in workmanship and functionality for a specified period of time. During this warranty period, we will address any issues or bugs that may arise, providing necessary support and maintenance.
